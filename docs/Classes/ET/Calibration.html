    Starts a calibration asyncronously, updating the state, point, currentPoint, quality and qualities properties.

    Declaration

    Swift

    public func start(points: [Int] = Points.Nine,
                  stopHID: Bool = true,
                  recalibrate: Bool = false,
                  width: Int32 = ET.Calibration.width,
                  height: Int32 = ET.Calibration.height)

    Parameters

    points

    Amount of calibration points. Pass one of ET.Calibration.Points.
    stopHID

    Tells Skyle to stop HID (Mouse positioning updates) during the calibration. Resets after the calibration has finished.
    recalibrate

    Tells the calibration to keep the connection open after the calibration has finished to perform a possible recalibration of certain points.
    width

    Screen width in pixels.
    height

    Screen height in pixels.
